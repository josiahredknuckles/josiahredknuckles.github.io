Garden of my Mind

(A poem I wrote the first time we met)


Estranged from my own skin

Would let me in

Where you begin is where i end

im falling all over again



Astray, so astray

When you call my name

Flowers bloom in this summer rain

Do you feel the same?



Is this real,

Is this true?

Call me down high on the moon

Somewhere between my mind and half past two

I found a love and it was made for you 



Weaving, between thoughts and limbs,

In you sinking deep, I cannot swim.

In these depths, the world falls dead.

You are my universe;the stars in my head



Is this real,

Is this true?

Call me down high on the moon

Somewhere between my mind and half past two

I found a love and it was made for you 



Wandering evermore, 

In the garden of my mind, you can call it yours.

Don't say a word, make no sound;

Heaven is here, heaven is now



Is this real,

Is this true?

Call me down high on the moon

Somewhere between my mind and half past two

I found a love and it was made for you
